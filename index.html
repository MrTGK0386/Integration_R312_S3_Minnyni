About me!

Hi, My name is Alice, I'm 20 and a self taught digital and traditional artist since 2020.

I am currently in an art school, so my style is still progressing, but I hope that if you like my work, you can leave me a tip on my Ko-fi or even commission me!

I'm pretty introverted so making my art for the public eyes was a huge jump, and I'm so glad that I did it because I've found some amazing people in the community and pushed myself to improve!
